SCHOLARSHIP

As we continue to fight against Covid, there are many who have suffered from loss. Loss of life, loss of income, loss of financial security, and the subsequent consequences of these losses. There are many families in which breadwinners lost their jobs due to the pandemic or breadwinners lost their lives to the pandemic. We are committed to ensuring that each child in need gets a fair chance at education and no adversity should result in a child having to discontinue their education. We are here to provide scholarship support to children and youth to continue their education and pursue their dreams.

Who is eligible for the scholarship?

  • Any child and youth between the ages of 4 and 21
  • Children where breadwinner(s) in the family have lost their job or lives

Chaanv Welfare is striving to sponsor school and college education for as many children from underprivileged backgrounds as possible by providing them with scholarships.
By making a small donation of Rs. 1,000, you can help us provide a better and a more secure future for an economically disadvantaged child.

Our mission – to become an empowered, effective, self-providing organisation and help as many humans as possible.
Our vision – deeply rooted in love and compassion, we embark ourself in providing service to children elders and women.
